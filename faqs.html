Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I contact customer support?

A: You can contact our customer support team by phone at 1-800-123-4567 or by email at support@supervolt.com.

Q: Do you offer international shipping?

A: Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries. Shipping fees and delivery times may vary depending on the destination.

Q: Are your batteries rechargeable?

A: Yes, the majority of our batteries are rechargeable. However, some specialty batteries may not be rechargeable.

Q: What is your return policy?

A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all purchases. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund within 30 days of receipt.

Q: How long do your batteries last?

A: The lifespan of our batteries varies depending on usage and environmental factors. However, on average, our batteries can last several years with proper care and maintenance.
